Mansa Leaf Productions

I'm Salif Soumahoro, I’m a director, videographer, and editor based in New York City, driven by a passion for storytelling through the visual medium. My goal is to create high-quality visuals that not only evoke emotion but also spark a call to action or inspire change. I specialize in beauty, sports, branding, and corporate work, but what truly defines my creative vision is a clean, empowering, and idealistic aesthetic. I believe the work I create should leave a lasting imprint, inspiring and motivating those who experience it. As a first-generation immigrant, my upbringing shapes this vision, fueling my desire to produce content that resonates deeply and drives transformation. At my core, I am a collaborative visionary, committed to elevating and inspiring those who seek meaningful change through the power of video.

About Me

I was born and raised in Harlem, NY, to immigrant parents from Ivory Coast. Growing up, positive influences were scarce, and inspiration seemed out of reach. But through media—whether it was cinema, sports, or even the fleeting commercials between shows—I discovered a world beyond my circumstances. These glimpses of greatness gave me the confidence to believe that change was possible. I started researching what makes people successful, and that curiosity fueled my education and creativity. I realized that through collaboration and determination, something powerful could be created—even from the humblest of beginnings. I wanted to be a beacon for those who felt hopeless or for those just starting their journey, to show them that greatness is achievable. I refuse to be confined by labels or limitations. Being boxed in creates doubt, and no one should ever feel limited. I push boundaries in every area of my life, and my mission is to help others do the same. Change is possible, and it starts with embracing the potential within, no matter where you come from.
